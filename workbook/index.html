Philosophy Workbook

This is a work of non-academic philosophy, by which I mean only that it is mostly about tools for thinking about a particular topic, in this case human social systems, in a way that I hope is useful to others who may wish to do the same thing.

It is a workbook in the sense that I am actively working on the thinking, by way of exercises ― workshops. At the end of each one, I try to learn and document some lessons and update the last section which is a reference manual for the tools.

This is very much a work in progress. I have barely started with laying out this website, though I have 90,000 words of content in my primary writing tool, which is Scrivener.

The top levels are intended (for now) to be a complete set. Lower heading levels that already have content can be reached by following their links in yellow. Those without links are placeholders until I am ready to publish them.

To dive right in, start with What is this work? to see what this workbook is about and where I hope to take it.
